My Tragic Backstory

I decided to look at world languages for this project because I majored in linguistics in undergrad. It was great to go back to my roots. A lot of linguistic field work involves carrying out research on lesser-known languages, languages which are not spoken by many communities and are often endangered. In my field methods class, I did research with a Zazaki speaker (a Kurdish language spoken in Turkey), so it was startling when (among other recognizable languages) I saw Zazaki in the endangered languages dataset (see the Bar Chart Prototype). Thanks to efforts by speakers like the one who was our field methods subject, that language is still only Vulnerable and not Definitely Endangered like so many others in the dataset.
